About the Career Fair

Our Institution

Our institution has been at the forefront of education and career development for over 25 years. We are committed to bridging the gap between academic excellence and industry requirements, ensuring our students are well-prepared for their professional journeys.

Our mission is to empower students and graduates with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities they need to succeed in their chosen careers. Through initiatives like the Virtual Career Fair, we create meaningful connections between talented individuals and leading organizations.

Career Fair History

2023

Successfully connected 500+ students with 50+ companies, resulting in 200+ job offers and internships.

2022

Expanded to include international companies and introduced virtual interview booths.

2021

Transitioned to a fully virtual format, reaching a wider audience of participants.
